Monterey Bay

In winter 2025, having just moved into a new apartment with nothing to decorate the walls, I was eager to create something to bring them a pop of color. Inspired by a recent trip to Monterey, and longing for the ocean, I created this map showing some of the towns and physical features around the bay.

Software used: QGIS, Inkscape
